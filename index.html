About Me

Alex’s community connection began with his childhood in Westchester. He loved growing up with the luxury of year-round sunshine, beaches, nightlife and family. He believes this is a city unlike any other—a beautiful melting pot of people and excellent food from all nations. People say you should love where you live, and Alex’s heart is truly in Miami.

During his award-winning career in the ultra-luxury travel industry, Alex gained years of experience assisting people plan and secure their dream voyages. He now uses these skills to help clients navigate the real estate market as they make one of their most important life decisions. Because of his strong local connections, Alex knows exactly which areas will best suit your unique needs.

In his free time, Alex enjoys trying new restaurants, mountain biking, and going to the beach with his wife of 10 years and adorable twin daughters.
